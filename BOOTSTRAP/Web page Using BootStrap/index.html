Stories of HOPE

The Everyday fights, efforts, and spirit of Chennai's hardworking people

AUTO DRIVER

From sunrise to midnight, Chennai's auto drivers navigate every street, rain or shine. They carry stories, smiles, and the heartbeat of the city with every ride. They work all day in traffic and heat, but still find it hard to earn enough for their families.

STREET VENDOR

They fill the city with colors, voices, and life — selling food, flowers, and dreams. Through heat and crowd, their energy keeps Chennai alive. They sell things on the streets with hope, but live in fear of losing their spot and their only income.

IT PROFESSIONALS

They chase deadlines, solve problems, and innovate — often from sunrise till midnight. Their energy powers the digital face of Chennai. They have good jobs, but too much stress and no time to rest or enjoy life.

CONSTRUCTION WORKERS

Brick by brick, they raise the skyline of Chennai. Their hard work stands tall, even when they remain unseen. They build our homes and roads, but their hard work is often ignored and poorly paid.
